About me

I am a PhD student supervised by Dr. Qing Wang and Dr. Marco Antonio Zúñiga Zamalloa at the Embedded System (ES) group, faculty of EEMCS, Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), The Netherlands. I also work very colsely with Dr. Jie Yang.

My research interest lies in combining AI techniques and edge computing to develop robust and trustworthy intelligent systems to solve practical problems in a collaborative, secure, and ubiquitous manner.
